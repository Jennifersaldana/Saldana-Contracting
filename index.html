History

Our construction business, Saldana Contracting, was established in 2015 with a vision to provide high-quality construction services in residential, commercial, and industrial sectors. Over the years, we have achieved numerous milestones, including the completion of over 500 projects, receiving multiple industry awards, and expanding our operations across several states.

Mission

At Saldana Contraction, our mission is to construct spaces that enhance quality of life. We are committed to delivering excellence through our unwavering dedication to quality, safety, and innovation. Our goal is to build lasting relationships with our clients based on trust, integrity, and superior service.

Our Locations

We are dedicated to meeting your remodeling needs and are willing to travel. We've worked in Missouri, Florida, and Louisiana, focusing on Cajun Country, Plantation Country, and Greater New Orleans. We're expanding our remodeling, painting, roofing, and bathroom services to Missouri, Florida, and Texas, bringing our expertise to more communities.

New Orleans

New Orleans, a vibrant city known for its rich culture and history, has been significantly impacted by hurricanes, most notably Hurricane Katrina in 2005. These natural disasters have caused widespread devastation, displacing residents and damaging infrastructure.
Our business specializes in disaster recovery and resilience services. We provide essential support such as rebuilding homes, restoring infrastructure, and offering financial assistance to help communities recover more swiftly and effectively.

Houma

Houma, Louisiana, was severely impacted by Hurricane Ida in 2021, suffering extensive damage to homes and infrastructure. Our business supports families in Houma by providing rebuilding services, financial assistance, and resources for long-term recovery. We help restore homes, ensuring they are more resilient against future storms, and work closely with local communities to support their rebuilding efforts.

Lake Charles

Lake Charles, Louisiana, faced severe damage from Hurricane Ida, affecting homes and infrastructure across the city. Our business helps families in Lake Charles by providing extensive rebuilding services, financial support, and vital recovery resources. We aim to make homes more resilient to future hurricanes and collaborate with the community to support their recovery and strengthen their long-term resilience.

Baton Rouge

Our roofing company operates in Baton Rouge, specializing in remodeling bathrooms and roofs. We provide high-quality renovation services to enhance the functionality and aesthetic appeal of homes. Our skilled team ensures durable and stylish upgrades, contributing to the comfort and value of residential properties in the Baton Rouge area.
